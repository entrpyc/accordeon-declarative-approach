Effect

Twice a day apply the lotion with a cotton pad on cleansed skin all over the face, avoiding eye contour area. In case of single breakouts apply the lotion 2-3 times a day only on these affected spots. Moisturizing cream, such as Acnaut Hydro Active Cream, and/or makeup can be applied at least 10-15 minutes after the lotion. To prevent ingrown hairs — apply the lotion right after the epilation and repeat after a few hours. Follow this routine in next couple of days.
